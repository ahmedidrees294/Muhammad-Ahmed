Overview

The Pakistani movie Sherdil is a 2022 action drama film directed by Sarmad Khoosat and produced by Momina Duraid. The film stars Mikaal Zulfiqar, Armeena Rana Khan, and Hassan Niazi. It tells the story of a young man named Haris Mustafa who dreams of becoming a fighter pilot. He faces many challenges along the way, but he never gives up on his dream.

The film begins with Haris's childhood. He is a bright and ambitious young boy who loves to fly. He dreams of one day becoming a fighter pilot for the Pakistan Air Force (PAF). However, his dream is met with resistance from his family. They believe that he is not cut out for such a dangerous career.

Despite his family's objections, Haris is determined to achieve his dream. He studies hard and gets accepted into the PAF Academy. He excels in his training and graduates at the top of his class. He is assigned to a fighter squadron and is soon flying missions against India.

Haris proves to be a skilled and courageous pilot. He shoots down several enemy aircraft and earns the respect of his fellow pilots. However, he also faces danger on a daily basis. He is shot down several times and nearly killed.

Despite the dangers, Haris continues to fly missions. He believes that it is his duty to protect his country. He also believes that he can make a difference in the world. He wants to show the world that peace is possible.

Sherdil is a patriotic film that celebrates the courage and determination of the Pakistani people. It is a story about hope and triumph over adversity. It is a film that will inspire viewers to never give up on their dreams.
