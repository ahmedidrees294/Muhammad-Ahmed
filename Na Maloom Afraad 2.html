Overview

Na Maloom Afraad 2 is a 2017 Pakistani action comedy film directed by Nabeel Qureshi and written by Fizza Ali Meerza. It is the sequel to the 2014 film Na Maloom Afraad. The film stars Fahad Mustafa, Javed Sheikh, Mohsin Abbas Haider, Urwa Hocane, Hania Amir, Nayyar Ejaz, Saleem Mairaj, and Nazar Hussain.

The film takes place a few years after the events of the first film. Farhan (Fahad Mustafa), Shakeel (Javed Sheikh), and Moon (Mohsin Abbas Haider) are now living in South Africa. Farhan is working as a security guard, Shakeel is a taxi driver, and Moon is a waiter. They are all struggling to make ends meet.

One day, Moon's girlfriend Parri (Hania Amir) tells him that her wealthy father is coming to South Africa. Parri's father, an Arab Sheikh (Nayyar Ejaz), is bringing a gold commode with him. Moon and his friends decide to steal the gold commode and sell it.

However, things don't go as planned. The gold commode is mixed up with Parri's wedding paraphernalia and ends up with Farhan, Shakeel, and Moon. They are now being chased by the Sheikh's goons.

Farhan, Shakeel, and Moon must use all of their skills and resources to escape from the Sheikh's goons and get the gold commode back to Parri. Along the way, they will have to deal with a variety of obstacles, including a corrupt police officer, a group of criminals, and a wild baboon.

Na Maloom Afraad 2 is a fun and action-packed comedy that is sure to entertain audiences of all ages. The film is full of laughs, suspense, and heart. It is a must-see for fans of the first film and anyone who loves a good comedy.
