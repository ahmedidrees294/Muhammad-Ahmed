Overview

Money Back Guarantee is a 2023 Pakistani crime comedy film written and directed by Faisal Qureshi. The film features an ensemble cast featuring Fawad Khan, Ayesha Omer, Hina Dilpazir, Ali Safina, Wasim Akram, Shayan Khan, Mikaal Zulfiqar, Gohar Rasheed, and Jan Rambo.

The film follows the story of a group of amateur thieves who decide to rob a bank in order to lift themselves out of their impoverished circumstances. The group is led by Fawad Khan, who plays a charming and charismatic con artist. The other members of the group include Ayesha Omer, who plays a naive and gullible young woman, Hina Dilpazir, who plays a tough and street-smart woman, and Ali Safina, who plays a loyal and bumbling sidekick.

The group of thieves faces a number of challenges as they plan and execute their heist. They must contend with a corrupt police officer, a skilled security guard, and a group of rival thieves. However, they are determined to succeed, and they eventually pull off the perfect heist.

Money Back Guarantee is a fun and entertaining film that is sure to leave you laughing. The film is well-acted, well-directed, and well-written. The cast is excellent, and the story is engaging. If you are looking for a good time, then I highly recommend checking out Money Back Guarantee.
