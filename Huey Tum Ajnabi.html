Overview

Huey Tum Ajnabi is a 2023 Pakistani romantic drama film directed by Kamran Shahid and produced by Riaz Shahid under the banner of Riaz Shahid Films. The film stars Mikaal Zulfiqar, Sadia Khan, Ayesha Omar, Alyy Khan, Sohail Ahmed, Mehmood Aslam, Shafqat Cheema, Samina Pirzada, and Shahid himself in lead and supporting roles. The film's music is composed by Shani Arshad, while the lyrics are penned by Sabir Zafar.

The film is set against the backdrop of the 1971 war. Junaid (Mikaal Zulfiqar) is a young man who belongs to a wealthy family. He is a carefree and fun-loving person who doesn't take life too seriously. Zara (Sadia Khan) is a young woman who comes from a middle- class family. She is a kind and compassionate person who is always willing to help others.

Junaid and Zara meet by chance and fall in love. However, their love is forbidden due to the ongoing war. Junaid's family disapproves of his relationship with Zara because they fear that she will be in danger if they stay together. Zara's family is also against her relationship with Junaid because they fear that he will not be able to provide for her.

Despite the obstacles, Junaid and Zara continue to love each other. They face many challenges, but they never give up on each other. In the end, they are able to overcome all of the obstacles and they live happily ever after.

The film has received mixed reviews from critics and audiences alike. Some critics have praised the film for its beautiful cinematography and its melodious music. However, some critics have criticized the film for its slow pace and its unrealistic portrayal of the 1971 war.

Overall, Huey Tum Ajnabi is a decent film that is worth watching for its beautiful cinematography and its melodious music. However, the film's slow pace and its unrealistic portrayal of the 1971 war may disappoint some viewers.
